The Best Movies According to Ankit

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Amazing Spider-Man

This is my favourite Holly-Wood Movie. I love the graphics and the animations.

3 Idiots.

"3 Idiots" is a heartwarming and humorous Indian film that cleverly critiques the education system while celebrating the importance of friendship and pursuing one's passions.

Ramaiya Vastavaiya

Super beautiful film. Really romantic.
